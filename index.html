Our Services

Cloud Storage & Access

Upload, organize, and access your data from anywhere in the world. Our ultra-secure cloud servers offer end-to-end encryption, high redundancy, and lightning-fast retrieval. Whether you're managing media files, documents, or archives — Unified Hub keeps them safe and instantly accessible.

Data Security & Compliance

Our platform complies with top industry standards (GDPR, ISO 27001). Each file is encrypted with AES-256-bit security, ensuring absolute confidentiality. We also provide user-level permission controls, 2FA, and automated breach detection.

AI File Organization

Our AI engine intelligently categorizes and tags your files for quick retrieval. Duplicate detection, smart search, and predictive sorting help teams save hours every week while maintaining perfect file hygiene.

Collaboration & Sharing

Empower your team to co-edit, comment, and review files in real-time. Assign permissions, track revisions, and integrate with Slack, Google Workspace, and Microsoft Teams for frictionless collaboration.

Backup & Disaster Recovery

Automatic backups every 24 hours, with real-time version history. In case of data loss, restore entire directories or individual files in seconds. Our distributed architecture ensures 99.99% uptime reliability.

About Unified Hub

Founded in 2023, Unified Hub began as a vision to revolutionize how digital assets are managed and shared. Today, we’re one of the fastest-growing cloud collaboration platforms, trusted by over 5,000 companies worldwide.


Our core philosophy centers around simplicity and security — combining cutting-edge technology with a user experience that feels effortless. We invest heavily in AI-driven automation, ensuring our clients never waste time searching or sorting files again.


We’re headquartered in San Francisco with offices across Europe and Asia, serving industries from fintech and healthcare to media production and education.
