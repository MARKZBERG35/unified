Practice Areas

Comprehensive legal services tailored to your needs. Each practice area is supported by experienced attorneys and a proven approach to achieve practical results.

Corporate & Business Law

We advise corporations, partnerships, and startups on formation, governance, mergers & acquisitions, commercial contracts, and shareholder matters. Our team helps clients anticipate risk, negotiate durable agreements, and execute transactions efficiently.

Typical matters:

  • Entity formation & governance
  • Mergers, acquisitions & asset purchases
  • Commercial contracts, NDAs, licensing
  • Corporate compliance & director duties

Clients receive tailored corporate playbooks, risk assessments, and hands-on support throughout negotiations and closings.

Real Estate Law

We represent owners, developers, lenders, and tenants in both transactional and litigation matters. From purchase agreements to title disputes and leasing, our team ensures outcomes that preserve value and mitigate exposure.

Typical matters:

  • Commercial purchase & sale agreements
  • Leasing negotiations & tenant advocacy
  • Title review, due diligence & escrow
  • Zoning, land use & development agreements

We partner with surveyors, title companies, and municipal counsel to provide cohesive real estate solutions.

Family Law

Our family law practice handles sensitive matters with discretion and clarity — including divorce, custody, spousal support, prenuptial agreements, and asset division. We prioritize clients’ long-term wellbeing in every negotiation and court appearance.

Typical matters:

  • Divorce and legal separation
  • Child custody and visitation
  • Spousal & child support calculations
  • Prenuptial and postnuptial agreements

We offer mediation-focused alternatives when appropriate, reducing cost and emotional strain while protecting rights.

Civil Litigation

From contract disputes to business torts and regulatory matters, our litigators craft persuasive strategies to pursue or defend claims in state and federal courts. We emphasize early case assessment and cost-effective dispute resolution.

Typical matters:

  • Breach of contract
  • Business torts and partnership disputes
  • Complex commercial litigation
  • Alternative dispute resolution (mediation/arbitration)

We build litigation plans focused on case value, timeline, and client objectives — from demand letters through trial and appeal.

Employment & Labor Law

We counsel employers and employees on workplace issues, policy development, and dispute resolution. Our practice covers wage & hour compliance, discrimination claims, wrongful termination defense, and employment agreements.

Typical matters:

  • Employee handbooks & workplace policies
  • Employment contracts and restrictive covenants
  • Discrimination and harassment claims
  • Internal investigations and regulatory defense

We help employers implement compliance programs and advise employees on enforcement of workplace rights.

Intellectual Property

Protecting innovations and creative assets is core to our IP practice. We assist clients with trademark clearance and registration, copyright matters, licensing agreements, and IP enforcement strategies.

Typical matters:

  • Trademark search and registration
  • Copyright registration and enforcement
  • Licensing and technology transfer
  • IP due diligence for transactions

We coordinate with patent agents when inventions require patent protection and offer holistic IP strategies aligned to business goals.
